ABOUT

Cascadia is a local gastropub that focuses on sustainability for the future and sourcing locally. Right now we are facing very scary and uncertain times. We can't thank you enough for all the support we have received so far. Please continue to be there for us as we get through this together. We've added some new features to the website that we would love for you to check out. There is a pickup order link that you can click and it will take you straight to our menu and you can easily place an order to be picked up. Or click the Uber eats icon to place an order for delivery. New customers to Uber eats will receive $10 off their 1st order. We have also started the "Cascadia At Your House" liquor, wine, and beer take out program. To see what's available check out our liquor store menu below. Thank you so much again, and we can't wait to see you all hopefully sooner than later! Stay healthy!

ABOUT

Cascadia Public House is a brand new establishment located at 6314 N Ash Street in Spokane. We are nestled right in the heart of the 5-mile area and are extremely excited to provide guests with the best locally sourced drinks and dishes combined with unmatched customer service. Brandishing a complete top to bottom makeover as well two amazing custom built fire pits on the patio, Cascadia Public House is the perfect place to hangout and spend your summer nights by the fire.
